### [From Mutual Information to Endogenous Viability](./blog/from-mutual-information-to-endogenous-viability/)
A long-form essay on a deceptively simple question: if an intelligent system must somehow represent the external world, can that be captured by mutual information alone? ### [Line Loss for Intelligence](./blog/line-loss-for-intelligence/)
A research note on a new question: as AI becomes planet-scale infrastructure (like the electric grid), "intelligence" is transported across space. Transporting control-relevant information has unavoidable thermodynamic and information-theoretic costs. Network topology (hierarchical / modular / fractal) may minimize those costs under physical constraints.
